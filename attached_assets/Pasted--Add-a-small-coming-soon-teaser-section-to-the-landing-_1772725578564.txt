Why This Works Hard For You
Three things happen with this section beyond just teasing a feature:
It collects emails. Even people who are not ready to sign up for the full product might drop their email for early access to Ask Watchloom. That is a warm lead list building itself passively.
It signals product ambition. A visitor sees that you are not just building a filing cabinet — you are building something they can have a conversation with. That raises the perceived ceiling of the product significantly.
It creates urgency around the free trial. If someone sees a compelling coming soon feature, they are more likely to sign up now so they are already an established user when it launches rather than starting from scratch later.
