Clear error: `there is no unique or exclusion constraint matching the ON CONFLICT specification`. The unique index `idx_comp_dim_status_unique` is a **partial index** (`WHERE workspace_id IS NOT NULL`), and PostgreSQL doesn't allow partial indexes to be used in ON CONFLICT clauses.

The fix is to change the ON CONFLICT to use a DO UPDATE without specifying the conflict target, or better — use a plain INSERT with a prior DELETE, or change the conflict target to the primary key.

Simplest fix — replace ON CONFLICT with a SELECT then INSERT or UPDATE pattern:
